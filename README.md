# 🏨 Hotel Visitors Analytics Dashboard – Tableau Project
An interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing hotel visitor trends, booking patterns, and performance metrics to generate actionable business insights.

🔗 **Live Interactive Dashboard:**  
[View on Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/ritesh.nalawade/viz/hotel-visitors-analysis/Dashboard1?publish=yes)

---

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is a comprehensive **Hotel Business Intelligence Dashboard** built using Tableau to analyze:

- Visitor trends  
- Revenue generation  
- Booking source performance  
- Room profitability  
- Customer satisfaction patterns  

The analysis is based on **5,475 historical records**, enabling meaningful business insights across multiple operational dimensions.

The objective of this project is to transform raw hospitality data into **actionable strategies** that improve revenue, marketing efficiency, and service quality.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The dashboard is designed to answer critical business questions such as:

- Which country generates the highest revenue?
- Which booking source is the most profitable?
- Which room categories contribute the most to profit?
- Which rooms have the lowest ratings and require improvement?
- What seasonal and day-wise trends impact revenue?
- How can discounts and marketing campaigns be optimized?

This project focuses on **decision-support analytics** rather than simple data visualization.

# 📊 Key Analysis Modules

## 🌍 Revenue by Country Analysis

- Revenue contribution by each country  
- Identification of high-value customer markets  
- Comparison of domestic vs international revenue  

### 💡 Business Impact:
Helps management focus marketing campaigns on high-performing countries and explore growth opportunities in underperforming regions.

---

## 📈 Booking Source Profitability Analysis

- Revenue generated by each booking source  
- Comparison of direct bookings vs third-party platforms  
- Profitability trend by booking channel  

### 💡 Business Impact:
Identifies the most profitable booking channels, allowing the hotel to:

- Increase investment in high-performing sources  
- Negotiate better commission structures  
- Improve direct booking strategies  

---

## 🛏 Room Category Profitability Analysis

- Revenue generated per room type  
- Occupancy rate comparison  
- Profit margin by room segment  

### 💡 Business Impact:
Enables the hotel to:

- Promote high-profit room categories  
- Adjust pricing strategy  
- Allocate marketing budgets effectively  

---

## ⭐ Room Rating & Performance Analysis

- Identification of lowest-rated rooms  
- Rating trends by room type  
- Customer satisfaction distribution  

### 💡 Business Impact:
Low-performing room categories can be:

- Investigated for service issues  
- Renovated or upgraded  
- Improved operationally to increase customer satisfaction and repeat bookings  

---

## 📅 Time-Based Trend Analysis

### Day-Wise Analysis
- Daily booking patterns  
- Weekday vs weekend revenue comparison  

### Month-Wise Analysis
- Seasonal occupancy trends  
- Revenue fluctuations across months  

### 💡 Business Impact:
Helps in:

- Identifying peak seasons  
- Applying discounts during low-demand periods  
- Planning staffing and inventory  
- Creating promotional campaigns strategically
